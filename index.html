Avoid the speed bumps on the road to fleet electrification. We’ll help you navigate the course from start to finish and beyond.

Inspiration is a purpose-built investment and infrastructure company advancing the electric vehicle (EV) transition. We partner with fleet businesses to finance, build, own and operate the real assets that enable a rapid and profitable deployment of electric vehicles. Whether converting an existing internal combustion engine (ICE) fleet or scaling an emerging EV-only business, having an aligned partner without ties to a specific product, technology, or financial structure allows you to pursue a strategy tailored to your unique needs, rather than what a vendor wants to sell. As your partner, we design and implement a custom solution that ensures your EV initiative is seamless. Whether you require a complete fleet-as-a-service solution or support with one or two key elements such as vehicle financing or charging, Inspiration can help.
