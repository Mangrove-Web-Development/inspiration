Get your EV mix right, right from the start

With a rapidly expanding list of vehicle makes and models to choose from, designing the optimal EV fleet that’s right for your business is critical for success.Features and benefits vary by model, new vehicles come to market all the time, and supply chains and inventory can be unpredictable. With Inspiration on your side, you’ll reduce risk by having the best intelligence and the right financing options supporting your success.

  • Use: Get the best vehicles to move your passengers, cargo, or both.
  • Range: Ensure that you have all the energy you need to complete trips without interruption.
  • Cost: Take full advantage of all incentives and minimize vehicle operating costs.
  • Access: Leverage our OEM relationships and proprietary channels to get vehicles more quickly and reliably.

Maximize value with incentives and financing

The list of incentives and rewards for owning EVs and installing charging infrastructure is long and growing. Inspiration keeps close tabs on all of them, so you don’t have to. Combined with our flexible financing structures that share any value received from subsidies or incentives, your EV fleet investment will generate more value with reduced risk.

  • Federal tax credit: Most EVs are eligible for a significant tax credit, and we actively monitor developments to stay ahead of the game and make sure we’re taking full advantage.

  • State tax credits: Many states offer tax credits or rebates for EV purchases - we make sure you don’t miss out.

  • Utility programs: Many electric utilities offer subsidies for EV purchases, special rates for EV charging, and other incentives. We design your EV fleet plan to capture all available benefits.

  • Environmental markets: ertain states have adopted policies enabling environmental credits for the use of EVs, such as California’s Low-Carbon Fuel Standard (“LCFS”). Eligibility can be complicated, and it's easy to miss out on potential value. As experts in environmental markets, we ensure you don't lose an opportunity for additional value.
