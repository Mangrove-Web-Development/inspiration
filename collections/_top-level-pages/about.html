Purpose-built to maximize EV and electric fleet value

The electrification of transportation and mobility is underway and will radically impact passenger fleet operators. Electrification is inevitable: a matter of when not if. But, frankly, we thought the transition would be farther along by now. After all, compared with gas-powered vehicles, EVs have vastly greater longevity and lower maintenance costs. Fleet electrification has the potential to deliver significantly better value to operators. So, what’s the hold-up?

The reality is that despite the crystal clear competitive advantages of fleet electrification, adopting EVs requires many complicated decisions involving traditionally siloed sectors. Operators must resolve vehicle selection and procurement issues, charging infrastructure, energy procurement and optimization, government regulations, incentive management, maintenance, and storage. 

Our founding partners joined forces with one purpose: to accelerate the EV transition. We brought together a team with the cross-functional experience and expertise to enable fleet operators to make the leap with the least risk possible,  the most return on investment, and the least friction possible. We plan to scale along with our customers by maximizing every advantage of the EV disruption to the fleet industry.  

Inspired? Let’s do this together.
